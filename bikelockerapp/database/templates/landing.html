Secure Bike Parking

Looking to securely park your bike at a transit station? You've come to the right place.

Cascade Bicycle Club manages King County Metro’s leased bike lockers at transit centers across the county. We can set you up with an available locker and will collect and hold your key deposit. Vacancies vary by location and openings change often. Reservable lockers are most suited to people who need a guaranteed space at a specific location where they plan to park their bike frequently, year-round, to catch transit.

Lease Now!

Leased Locker Sign Up:

Find a locker

Locate a locker that you would like to lease below by filtering for the specific city’s transit locations.

Fill out inquiry form

Select one or multiple locations that you are interested in. Cascade Bicycle Club will reach out with furthur steps to approve you for your locker! If there are no available spots, you will be placed on the waitlist and notified when a spot is open.

Sign agreement & pay deposit

Cascade Bicycle Club will send a lease. You’ll need to fill out, sign, and return the lease along with a $50 key deposit. The deposit will be refunded to you when the lease is terminated and the key has been returned.

Recieve your key and start biking

Once the onboarding process is finished you will recieve your locker key in the mail and are ready to start using your locker!

Have trouble finding a location you wanted?

Follow the link here to view all bike locker locations in King County.

Please note that this list is provided lists ALL bike locker locations in the county, including locations run by King County Metro (which are managed by Cascade Bicycle Club) and also locations run by Sound Transit which are not.

If you are interested in a Sound Transit location, you will not be leasing through Cascade Bicycle Club and must follow their separate instructions.
