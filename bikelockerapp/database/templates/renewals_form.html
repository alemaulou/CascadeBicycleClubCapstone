Thank you for renting a bike locker from King County Metro! Please answer all of the following questions so that we can process your renewal status in a timely manner - this survey will only take about 3 minutes. All answers are fully confidential.

If you are using your locker less than two times per week, please send your key back to us so that we can lease your locker to someone else. Directions for returning your key are given after completing the form. Once the key is received, we will return your initial key deposit.
